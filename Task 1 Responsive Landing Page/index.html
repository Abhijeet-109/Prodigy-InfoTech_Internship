About BMW


Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, known as BMW, is a German multinational corporation which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. The company is renowned worldwide for its premium engineering, distinctive design, and relentless innovation.

Founded in 1916, BMW has grown into one of the most iconic automotive brands, known for delivering the "Ultimate Driving Machine". With a commitment to electric mobility and cutting-edge technology, BMW is shaping the future of transportation.
